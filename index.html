Weekend

By Christine Sherk | Photos by Kelly Lyon | The Register-Guard
Oct. 3, 2015

Starting in October, Halloween feels as if it’s just around the corner what with all the offerings local farms provide for us to make the most of this harvest-filled and ghoulish season.

And as more and more people get into the decorating swing of things, adorning porches and homes with festive, even monsterish, fall décor, it’s a great time of year to feel like a kid again. Everyone deserves pumpkins, after all.

The weekends especially — there are five weekends in October this year, folks — yield enough time to pack up the family, head to your favorite farm and make a day of experiencing some good ol’ country fun while supporting our hardworking local growers.

From U-pick pumpkin patches to corn mazes, from fall décor to horse-drawn hayrides, we provide the basics so you can head out with a solid plan.

We provide a couple other options just right for this time of year, too. Happy harvesting!
